Getting Started

How does Propcash work?

Propcash is a marketplace that connects property sellers with a network of 500+ vetted cash investors. When you submit your property, we distribute it to investors who specialize in your area and property type. Multiple investors compete by submitting offers, which drives your price up. Our Marketplace Specialists review all offers and present you with the best options.

Is Propcash free to use?

Yes, absolutely. Propcash is 100% free for sellers. We don't charge any fees, commissions, or hidden costs. Our revenue comes from a referral fee paid by the investor when a deal closes‚Äînever from you.

Am I obligated to accept an offer?

No. There is zero obligation to accept any offer you receive. If the offers don't meet your needs or expectations, you can simply walk away‚Äîno fees, no pressure, no hard feelings.

What information do I need to get started?

Just basic information about your property: address, property type, condition, and your contact information. The entire process takes about 2 minutes. We'll follow up with a few additional questions to help investors make accurate offers.

Timeline & Process

How quickly will I receive offers?

Most properties receive their first offer within 24 hours. Our network of 500+ investors is actively looking for properties, so response times are typically very fast. The average property receives 3+ competing offers.

How long does it take to close?

The average closing timeline is 14 days, but we've completed transactions in as little as 3 days. The timeline is flexible and depends on your needs‚Äîsome sellers prefer longer closings (30-90 days) to coordinate their move.

What happens after I submit my property?

Once you submit your property, we immediately distribute it to relevant investors in our network. As offers come in, our Marketplace Specialists review them for price, timeline, and investor reputation. We then present you with the best options and guide you through the decision-making process.

Can I choose my closing date?

Yes. Different investors offer different closing timelines, giving you flexibility. Whether you need to close in 3 days or 90 days, we work with you to find an investor whose timeline matches your needs.

Property & Offers

What types of properties do you accept?

We work with all residential property types: single-family homes, condos, townhomes, multi-family properties, and even land. Properties can be in any condition‚Äîfrom move-in ready to severe disrepair.

Do I need to make repairs before selling?

No. All investors in our network buy properties "as-is," meaning you don't need to make any repairs, upgrades, or improvements. They'll make offers based on the current condition of your property.

How are offers determined?

Each investor evaluates your property independently based on location, condition, market comparables, and their investment criteria. The marketplace model creates competition, which naturally drives offers higher than you'd get from a single cash buyer.

What if I don't receive any offers?

While this is rare, if your property doesn't receive offers within 48 hours, we'll reach out to discuss alternative options and next steps. This might include adjusting expectations, exploring different sale methods, or connecting you with local resources.

Can I list my property if it has liens or back taxes?

Yes. Many investors in our network specialize in properties with liens, back taxes, or other title issues. These complications are factored into their offers, and they often handle the resolution process as part of the sale.

Costs & Fees

Are there any fees or commissions?

No. Sellers pay zero fees to use Propcash. Unlike traditional real estate sales (which typically cost 6% in agent commissions), our service is completely free. The investor pays us a referral fee when a deal closes.

Will I pay closing costs?

This depends on the offer. Many investors cover all closing costs as part of their offer, while others may ask you to contribute. This information is clearly disclosed in each offer so you can compare total net proceeds.

How do cash offers compare to traditional sales?

Cash offers are typically lower than retail market value (usually 70-90% depending on condition), but you save 6% in agent commissions, avoid repair costs, and eliminate holding costs like mortgage payments, utilities, and insurance. For many sellers, the net proceeds are comparable‚Äîand you close much faster.

Comparing Your Options

How is Propcash different from "We Buy Houses" companies?

Traditional "We Buy Houses" companies give you one offer‚Äîusually a lowball. Propcash creates competition by sending your property to 500+ investors. Multiple competing offers drive your price up, often $25,000+ higher on a $300,000 property.

Should I list with an agent instead?

It depends on your situation. Traditional listings work well if you have time (3-6 months), your property is in good condition, and you can handle showings and repairs. Propcash works better if you need speed, want to avoid repairs, or can't wait for buyer financing. There's no conflict‚Äîyou can try both approaches simultaneously.

Can I list on Propcash and with an agent at the same time?

Yes, as long as you haven't signed an exclusive listing agreement with an agent. Many sellers explore both options to see which yields better results. Just make sure you're transparent with all parties involved.
