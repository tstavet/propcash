The Nashville Real Estate Market in 2025: What You Need to Know

Nashville's real estate market has experienced tremendous growth over the past decade, transforming Music City into one of the hottest housing markets in the United States. With an influx of new residents, rising home prices, and increased competition, understanding the current market landscape is crucial for homeowners looking to sell.

Current Nashville Housing Market Statistics

As of early 2025, the Nashville metropolitan area housing market shows the following trends:

  • Median home price: Approximately $425,000 (up 8% from 2024) [Source: Zillow]
  • Average days on market: 35-45 days for traditional listings [Source: Realtor.com]
  • Inventory levels: Still below historical averages, creating a seller's market in many neighborhoods [Source: Redfin]
  • Price per square foot: Ranging from $180-$350+ depending on location and condition [Source: NAR]
  • Buyer demand: Strong, particularly for move-in ready homes in desirable neighborhoods [Source: FRED Economic Data]

Market data sources: Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, National Association of Realtors, and Federal Reserve Economic Data. Data is subject to change and should be verified for current accuracy.
