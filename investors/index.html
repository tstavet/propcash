Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our network

What are the requirements to join?

We require proof of funds or financing capability, a track record of closing deals (or equivalent real estate experience), and the ability to close quickly. We vet all investors to maintain marketplace quality.

How much does it cost to join?

Pricing varies by market and demand. We offer monthly subscription plans that give you access to all deals in your target areas. Contact us for specific pricing in your markets of interest.

How many investors compete for each deal?

On average, properties receive 3-5 offers from our network. We carefully manage investor density to ensure everyone gets quality deal flow without excessive competition.

Do you share my offers with other investors?

No. Your offer amounts and deal structures remain confidential. Sellers see competing offer counts and ranges, but never specific investor identities or exact figures.

What types of properties are available?

We work with all residential property types: single-family homes, condos, townhomes, multi-family properties, and land. Properties range from retail-ready to heavy rehab opportunities.

Can I operate in multiple markets?

Yes. Set criteria for as many markets as you want. You'll receive notifications for all properties matching your parameters across all your target areas.
