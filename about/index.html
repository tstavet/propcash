🌲 About Propcash

We're Changing How America Sells Homes

Propcash was founded on a simple belief: selling your home shouldn't be complicated, stressful, or unfair. We're here to provide homeowners with a faster, fairer alternative to the traditional real estate process.

Our Story

Propcash began when our founders experienced firsthand how difficult and frustrating it can be to sell a property through traditional channels. Endless showings, costly repairs, uncertain timelines, and agent commissions that eat into your profit — there had to be a better way.

We started with a mission to create a home buying company that homeowners could actually trust. No high-pressure tactics. No lowball offers. No hidden fees. Just honest, transparent transactions that respect both the seller's time and their financial situation.

Today, we've helped hundreds of families across the United States sell their homes quickly and move on to their next chapter with cash in hand and peace of mind.

Our founders wanted to make home selling feel less like a business transaction and more like working with a trusted friend. That's where I come in! 🌲

Our Simple Process

We've streamlined home selling into three simple steps:

1

You Contact Us

Call us at (856) 938-5550, fill out our online form, or send us a text. Tell us about your property and your situation. This takes about 5 minutes and there's zero obligation.

2
