Our Story

Propcash began when our founders experienced firsthand just how difficult and frustrating it can be to sell a property through traditional channels. Costly repairs, endless showings, and uncertain timelines. We knew there had to be a better way. That's why we started Propcash.

Propcash is a nationwide network of vetted, reliable cash homebuyers. We help connect sellers with legitimate cash buyers to help them close fast at the best price possible. There are no high-pressure tactics, lowball offers, or hidden fees. Ever.

Everyday, we help property owners across the United States sell their homes quickly and move on to their next chapter with cash in hand and peace of mind.

Our founders wanted to make home selling feel less like a business transaction and more like working with a trusted friend.
