The Problem We Solve

Trying to sell your home quickly is hard. Most "we buy houses" companies offer 30-40% below market value, with no explanation of how they arrive at those numbers.

There's also a major lack of competition. When sellers only work with one or two buyers, they have no leverage or way to know if the price is fair.

That's when we realized: the cash home buying industry needed a marketplace.

How we make money (and why it matters)

Transparency is core to what we do, so let's be clear about our business model:

Sellers pay nothing

There are no costs for sellers. Not to submit their property, not to receive offers, not when you close.

Investors only pay when a deal is reached

Investors pay an industry standard fee, but only when they successfully close on an attractive property through our marketplace.

Why This Model Works Better

Because we only get paid when deals close successfully, our incentives are aligned with our members. We're motivated to create deals that align with both sellers and investors.
