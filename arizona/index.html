Most Arizona sellers receive multiple cash offers within 24-48 hours. Once you accept an offer, closing typically takes 7-14 days through a title company. No drawn-out listing periods or waiting for buyer financing.

Which Arizona cities do you cover?

Our investor network covers all of Arizona, including Phoenix, Tucson, Mesa, Scottsdale, Chandler, Gilbert, and smaller markets throughout the state. If you have a property in Arizona, we have investors interested.

Why are investors so interested in Arizona properties?

Arizona adds 200+ new residents daily and home values have appreciated 54% over 5 years. Tech giants like Intel and TSMC are expanding, creating strong job growth and housing demand.

Do I need to make repairs before selling?

No. Our investors buy Arizona properties as-is. Whether your home needs roof repairs, AC updates, or pool maintenance, you'll receive cash offers based on current condition. No repairs needed.

Are there any fees or commissions?

Zero fees. Unlike traditional real estate sales where you'd pay 5-6% in agent commissions plus closing costs, Propcash charges nothing. The offer you accept is the amount you receive.

How is Propcash different from "We Buy Houses" companies?

Traditional cash buyers make you a single lowball offer. Propcash is a marketplace where 500+ investors compete for your property. Competition drives prices up, not down. You choose from multiple offers.

What about Arizona's SPDS disclosure?

Arizona requires a Seller's Property Disclosure Statement (SPDS). We'll guide you through completing this simple form. Our investors are familiar with Arizona requirements and the process is straightforward.

Can I sell my Arizona rental property with tenants?

Yes. Many of our investors specifically seek tenant-occupied properties. Sell your rental as-is without disrupting tenants or waiting for leases to expire.
