Common Questions We Get

Quick answers to help you get started faster.

How quickly will I hear back? +

During business hours (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm CST), you'll typically hear back within 8-15 minutes via text or phone, and within 2-4 hours via email. Outside of business hours, we'll respond first thing the next morning.

What information should I have ready? +

Just basic details about your property: address, condition, and your timeline. That's enough to get started. We'll walk you through anything else we need during our conversation.

Will I be pressured to accept an offer? +

Absolutely not. Our job is to create a marketplace and present you with options. You're in complete control. Many people reach out just to explore their options, and that's perfectly fine with us.

Can I schedule a call for a specific time? +

Yes! Text us at (615) 552-4296 with your preferred time and we'll schedule a call that works for your schedule. We're flexible and happy to accommodate.

Do you work with people nationwide? +

Yes! Our investor network covers all 50 states. No matter where your property is located, we can connect you with qualified buyers in your area.

What if I just have a quick question? +

Text us! It's the fastest way to get quick answers. Our team monitors texts throughout the day and you'll typically get a response within 8 minutes during business hours.
