Common Questions

Texas Home Selling FAQ

How fast can I sell my Texas house?

Most Texas sellers receive multiple cash offers within 24-48 hours. Once you accept an offer, closing typically takes 7-14 days through a title company. No drawn-out listing periods or waiting for buyer financing.

Which Texas cities do you cover?

Our investor network covers all of Texas, including Dallas-Fort Worth, Houston, San Antonio, Austin, and smaller markets throughout the state. If you have a property in Texas, we have investors interested.

Why are investors so interested in Texas properties?

Texas has no state income tax, attracts 100+ corporate relocations annually, and has strong population growth. Investors pay premium prices knowing Texas properties offer both appreciation and strong rental returns.

Do I need to make repairs before selling?

No. Our investors buy Texas properties as-is. Whether your home needs foundation work (common in Texas clay soil), roof repairs, or cosmetic updates, you'll receive cash offers based on current condition.

Are there any fees or commissions?

Zero fees. Unlike traditional real estate sales where you'd pay 5-6% in agent commissions plus closing costs, Propcash charges nothing. The offer you accept is the amount you receive.

How is Propcash different from "We Buy Houses" companies?

Traditional cash buyers make you a single lowball offer. Propcash is a marketplace where 500+ investors compete for your property. Competition drives prices up, not down. You choose from multiple offers.

What about Texas property taxes?

Texas property taxes are handled at closing through the title company. Taxes are prorated so you only pay for the portion of the year you owned the property. We handle all the paperwork.

Can I sell my Texas rental property with tenants?

Yes. Many of our investors specifically seek tenant-occupied properties. Sell your rental as-is without disrupting tenants or waiting for leases to expire.
