Florida Home Selling FAQ

How fast can I sell my Florida house?

Most Florida sellers receive multiple cash offers within 24-48 hours. Once you accept an offer, closing typically takes 7-14 days through a title company. No waiting for buyer financing or insurance approvals.

Which Florida cities do you cover?

Our investor network covers all of Florida, including Tampa, Orlando, Jacksonville, Miami, Fort Lauderdale, and smaller markets throughout the state. If you have a property in Florida, we have investors interested.

What about Florida's insurance challenges?

Rising insurance costs make traditional sales difficult—buyers struggle to get coverage. Cash investors handle insurance themselves, so you avoid deals falling through due to insurance issues. It's one of the biggest advantages of selling to our investor network.

Do I need to make repairs before selling?

No. Our investors buy Florida properties as-is. Whether your home has hurricane damage, roof issues, or just needs updates, you'll receive cash offers based on current condition. No repairs needed.

Are there any fees or commissions?

Zero fees. Unlike traditional real estate sales where you'd pay 5-6% in agent commissions plus closing costs, Propcash charges nothing. The offer you accept is the amount you receive.

How is Propcash different from "We Buy Houses" companies?

Traditional cash buyers make you a single lowball offer. Propcash is a marketplace where 500+ investors compete for your property. Competition drives prices up, not down. You choose from multiple offers.

Can I sell my Florida vacation rental?

Yes. Many of our investors specifically seek short-term rental properties. Sell your vacation rental as-is, with or without existing bookings. We can work around your rental calendar.

What if my property is in a flood zone?

We buy properties in all flood zones. Our investors are experienced with Florida's flood requirements and factor this into their offers. You won't be penalized or rejected for flood zone location.
